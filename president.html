Message from the College President

  • President's Message
Gadsden State’s highly professional faculty and staff and a broad array of educa-tional offerings are available to assist you in meeting your educational, career and personal goals. Whether you’re interested in earning an associate’s degree, preparing for a four-year degree, pursuing training for business, industry or health related professions, enhancing a specific skill, or enjoy being a lifelong learner, we can help.

We serve students from all walks of life. At six convenient locations we offer a variety of low cost, flexible classes designed to meet your needs—including day and night classes with traditional classroom, on-line, hybrid, or directed study options. You’ll also enjoy an active and engaged campus life, filled with oppor-tunities to learn and grow.

Choose Your Direction. You’ve already done the first part by choosing Gadsden State—we’ll help you do the rest!
